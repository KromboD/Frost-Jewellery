Artist Info

George Frost is a local up and coming Hawke’s Bay jeweller.
George began his journey studying at the Peter Minturn Goldsmith College for 2 years located in Auckland, New Zealand.
Once completing his qualification, George set his sights on taking his knowledge abroad.

George then spent 2 years honing his craft at The Great Frog in London,
allowing himself to specialize in bespoke ring making and men’s jewellery.
After his time in London, George came back home to Hawke’s Bay in order to
build his homegrown skills and continues to learn the art of jewellery making for a variety of different clients and occasions.
